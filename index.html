Northern Lights Guide

When to go out

KP ≥ 3 and clear skies. Best window 9 PM – 2 AM. Check yr.no for Tromsø cloud cover before heading out.

Best spots

20+ min drive from the city. Ersfjordbotn beach or Kvaløya island. Away from streetlights is everything.

Camera settings

ISO 1600–3200 · f/2.8 or lower · 8–15 sec exposure. Tripod is non-negotiable. Shoot RAW if possible.

Apps

My Aurora Forecast (iOS/Android) · Space Weather Live · yr.no for Tromsø cloud cover.
