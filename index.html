🌌 Northern Lights Guide

When to go

Check KP widget. Go out when KP ≥ 3. Best window: 9 PM – 2 AM. Clear skies required — check yr.no.

Best spots

Drive 20+ min from city. Ersfjordbotn beach, Kvaløya island. Away from streetlights is key.

Camera settings

ISO 1600–3200 · f/2.8 or lower · 8–15 sec exposure · Tripod essential. Shoot RAW if possible.

Apps to use

My Aurora Forecast (iOS/Android) · Space Weather Live · yr.no for Tromsø clouds.
